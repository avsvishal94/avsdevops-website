About Vishal

Vishal is a DevOps architect and cloud infrastructure specialist with over 7+ years of experience building, scaling, and securing production systems at enterprise scale. He holds an M.Tech degree from IIT Kanpur and has worked across startups and Fortune 500 companies, designing CI/CD pipelines, multi-cloud architectures, and Kubernetes platforms that serve millions of users.

His passion for teaching stems from a belief that DevOps education should be practical, not theoretical. Every module in the AVSDevOps curriculum is built around real-world scenarios he has encountered in production — from debugging failing deployments at 3 AM to designing zero-downtime migration strategies.

With AVSDevOps, Vishal has trained over 100 engineers, many of whom have gone on to land roles at top tech companies, consulting firms, and innovative startups. His teaching philosophy is simple: build it, break it, fix it, ship it.
